- Tesla, Inc. filed a Form 8-K with the SEC on January 2, 2024.
- The report includes Tesla's vehicle production and delivery numbers for Q4 2023 and the entire year.
- Q4 2023 saw approximately 495,000 vehicles produced and over 484,000 delivered.
- Yearly figures for 2023 show a 38% YoY growth in deliveries to 1.81 million and a 35% YoY increase in production to 1.85 million.
- The majority of the production and deliveries in 2023 were Model 3/Y vehicles.
- Tesla will release its full financial results for Q4 and the full year 2023 on January 24, 2024, followed by a live Q&A webcast.
- The financial performance, including net income and cash flow, will be detailed in the upcoming earnings announcement.
- Tesla emphasizes that vehicle delivery numbers are only one aspect of the company's financial performance.
